01. Constraints & Boundaries

Requirements and Specifications

Functional Requirements

  • The system shall be manually triggered by the operator to initiate each cycle.
  • The cobot shall move to the ASRS2 rack/track position and align with the pallet for pickup.
  • The end-effector shall securely grip and lift pallets up to 500 g without slippage.
  • The cobot shall transfer the pallet from ASRS2 to the unloading position near ASRS1.
  • The system shall empty the cubes from the pallet into a collection box.
  • The cobot shall ensure complete emptying of the pallet before proceeding.
  • After unloading, the cobot shall transport the empty pallet to the ASRS1 track.
  • The end-effector shall place the empty pallet onto ASRS1 with repeatable positioning accuracy of ±2 mm.
  • The end-effector shall release the pallet on command within ≤ 1 second.
  • The system shall reset to initial position and wait for the next manual trigger.

Non-Functional Requirements

  • The system shall operate in a repeatable manual cycle, ensuring consistent performance.
  • The total weight of the end-effector shall not exceed 2 kg.
  • The system shall ensure safe operation, preventing accidental dropping of pallets or cubes.
  • The design shall be compact with approximate dimensions of 500 mm × 150 mm × 100 mm (subject to refinement).
  • The system shall maintain stable gripping during robot motion.
  • The system shall operate within ambient conditions of 20°C to 40°C.

Project Specifications

Technical parameters and system configuration details
  • 1 The end-effector shall handle pallets with a maximum load of 500 g, measured during lifting from ASRS2.
  • 2 The gripping system shall prevent slippage of the pallet under motion up to 1 m/s, verified during transfer operation.
  • 3 The end-effector shall have a total mass not exceeding 2 kg, measured after complete assembly.
  • 4 The system shall achieve pallet release within ≤ 1 second, measured from command input to release.
  • 5 The system shall achieve positioning accuracy of ±2 mm, measured over multiple pick-and-place cycles.
  • 6 The cobot shall transfer the pallet between ASRS2 and ASRS1 along a predefined path without loss of grip.
  • 7 The system shall empty the pallet completely into the collection box, verified by visual inspection after each cycle.
  • 8 The mounting interface shall be compatible with the TM5-700 Cobot flange, allowing installation without modification.
  • 9 The overall dimensions of the end-effector shall be approximately 500 mm × 150 mm × 100 mm, subject to design refinement.
  • 10 The system shall operate under ambient temperature conditions of 20°C to 40°C without performance degradation.
  • 11 The system shall support repeated manual cycles with consistent performance during operation.
