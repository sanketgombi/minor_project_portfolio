Conceptual Design

Concept 1: Magnetic Gripper

Magnetic Gripper

By Sanket | Magnetic

Uses powerful permanent or electro-magnets to lock onto metallic plates on the pallets. Very strong grip, but limited to magnetic components.

Concept 2: Mechanical Gripper

Mechanical Gripper

By Kridey | Mechanical

A motor-driven link clamping assembly to grip the pallet sides. Offers high clamping force, but is heavy and requires active power.

Concept 3: Self-Locking Gripper

Self-Locking Gripper

By Maitri | Self-Locking

Features a geometric toggle linkage that locks mechanically under payload weight, preventing accidental drops if power fails.

Concept 4: Fork Type Gripper

Fork Type Gripper

By Ayush | Passive

A passive pronged fork design that slides underneath the pallet channels. Simple and reliable, but requires highly precise alignment.
