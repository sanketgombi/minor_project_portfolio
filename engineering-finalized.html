Optimized Magnetic Gripper

The finalized Magnetic Gripper configuration integrates powerful switchable permanent and electro-magnet arrays with an ISO-standardized wrist flange. This design guarantees solid hold alignment and high speed cycles for pallet components up to 500 g, ensuring fail-safe operations.

  • ✓ Standard ISO 9409-1-50-4-M6 wrist interface adapter ensures direct bolt coupling.
  • ✓ Electro-permanent magnet cells consume power only during toggle engagement pulses.
  • ✓ Passive alignment pins ensure precise docking tolerances within ASRS boundaries.
