Timeline Details & Deliverables

Month 1

Research & Setup

Literature search and patent reviews to benchmark performance criteria. Selection of target storage bins and initial workspace dimension mappings.

Month 2

Concept & CAD

Evaluating four design concepts via Pugh Matrix. Detailed 3D CAD modeling of the chosen Hybrid Soft-Claw in SolidWorks. Finite Element Analysis on jaw displacement.

Month 3

Build & Circuit

3D printing structural links in PLA and finger pads in TPU. Actuator and FSR sensor wiring. Initial visual control scripts in TM Flow for shelf landmark tracking.

Month 4

Tests & Verify

Integrated redocking trials at the receiving rack. Force validation tests. Optimization of closed-loop control dynamics. Final documentation and demo setup.
