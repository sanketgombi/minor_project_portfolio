Simulink Closed-Loop Performance

Closed-loop positional transient dynamics of the linear actuator were simulated in MATLAB Simulink. The simulation validated response stability under maximum load bounds (up to 1.5 kg).

  • ✓ Settling Time: 0.82 seconds under maximum payload inertia loads.
  • ✓ Steady State Error: less than 0.5% positional offset limit.
  • ✓ Maximum Design Overshoot: restricted under 3.8% to protect objects.
